Hello! I'm Aseem.







About Me




I'm an undergraduate student at UCLA studying Computer Science and Engineering. I find great joy in immersing myself in the world of computing and constantly learning more about how computers function. To me, computer science provides some of the most fulfilling experiences - whether it's by creating or contributing to an app, finally surmounting a technical challenge that I've been hammering at for days, or just learning a new concept that gets me ever closer to understanding just how these machines work. I am currently working on growing my full stack skills through my various projects.


Outside the world of computer science, I enjoy playing video games, playing the tabla (a type of hand drum), daydreaming, creating interesting recipes, and juggling.
